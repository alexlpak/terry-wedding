We're Getting Married!

Kay and David met in the spring of 2013 in the first class David attended at Del City High School.

Throughout high school, they remained friends. They kept in contact in college and remained friends but did not communicate frequently. In the Spring of 2018, they met at the wedding of their mutual friends.

After meeting at the wedding, they spent many evenings hanging out with each other and talking on the phone. Soon they made it “Facebook official” and began attending church together, spending time with each other’s family, and even got a cat named Doc. Both Kay and David graduate from university in December, and will be pursuing their master's degrees in the fall of 2020.
